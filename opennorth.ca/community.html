Community

Open North is an active participant in the open government and open data communities. We're passionate about making democracy better through increased accesss to information and civic engagement. In addition to our projects and services, we're active members in a few groups and committees which strive for similar ideals. Our staff are also often invited to speak on topics related to open government data standards and online civic engagment.

To learn more about our work in the community, join our mailing list for updates

Groups and Committees

  • Table de concertation sur les données ouvertes de Montréal
    Open North joined the Table de concertation in April of 2012. The purpose of the Table is to discuss the evolution of open data in Montréal, with 6 people from the City and 6 from the civil society. As an active actor of the table, Open North has submitted lists of datasets that should be released and has proposed changes in the way the open data policy is followed by the city.
  • W3C Government Linked Data (GLD) Working Group
    Open North's Executive Director, James McKinney, joined this working group as an invited expert. The GLD group works to develop standards which help governments publish their data as effective and usable Linked Data, using Semantic Web technologies
